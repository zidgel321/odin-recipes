Egg Nog Ice cream

index

This is a easy egg nog ice cream recipe made in a ice cream maker

Ingredients

  • 3 cups raw whole milk
  • 8 whole eggs
  • 1 cup Irish cream Liqueur or 1 can condensed milk
  • 1 cup sugar or maple syrup
  • Fresh grated nugmeg to taste

Instructions

  1. Whisk or blend eggs in large bowl or blender with milk, sugar, and nutmeg for a few minutes until frothy
  2. Add mix to ice cream maker
  3. Ice cream may be eaten immediately as soft serve or transferred to freezer for a firmer texture.
  4. Add another dash of nutmeg to serve

Notes

  • Try serving with mini candy canes or choclate chips
