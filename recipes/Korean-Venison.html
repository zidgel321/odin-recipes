Korean Beef

Home

Ingredients

  • 1 LB ground Beef or Venison
  • 2 teaspoons microplaned Ginger
  • 5 cloves Garlic
  • 1/4 cup Soy Sauce mixed with 1/4c water
  • 3 Tablespoons Honey
  • green onions
  • Salt and Pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Brown the venison in a skillit
  2. Add the Ginger and Garlic and cook for a few minutes
  3. Add the soy sauce and honey
  4. Simmer for 10 min unti most of the liquid is gone
  5. Serve over rice of choice with lots of green onions
